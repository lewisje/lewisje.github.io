These links will test whether the process-isolation benefits of rel="noopener" exist when the window's opener property is set to null, whether by the opening page or by the opened page.

  • Open a new window without setting rel="noopener" or otherwise modifying the opener property.
  • Open a new window, first setting its opener property to null.
  • Open a new window that sets its own opener property to null.
  • Open a new window, setting rel="noopener".
